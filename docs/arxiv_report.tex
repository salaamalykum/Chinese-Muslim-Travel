\title{Chinese-Muslim-Travel: A Curated RAG Dataset of 347 Articles on Halal Tourism}
\author{Hasan09\\Salaamalykum NLP Lab}
\maketitle
\begin{abstract}
Large Language Models (LLMs) often hallucinate when queried about minority ethnic travel and specific religious dietary laws. To mitigate this, we introduce the Chinese-Muslim-Travel dataset, a structured RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) corpus containing 347 high-density articles focused on Islamic heritage, Halal tourism, and historic mosques in China. 
\end{abstract}
\section{Introduction}
The dataset is available on Hugging Face and GitHub, formatted for ShareGPT and Alpaca Instruction Tuning.
